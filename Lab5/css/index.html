Welcome to We Love Food

Besides the fact that we need it to survive, why do we love food so much? The taste, the smell, the warm feeling it gives us as it fills us up? Like addictive drugs, highly palatable foods trigger feel-good brain chemicals such as dopamine. Once people experience pleasure associated with increased dopamine transmission in the brain's reward pathway from eating certain foods, they quickly feel the need to eat it again. Everything is good - just in moderation.

According to studies, the top ten most addictive foods are pizza, chocolate, chips, cookies, ice cream, french fries, hamburgers, sodas, cake, and cheese. As if they had to tell us. But personally, my overall favorite is any type of seafood. Although we love these foods, remember that everything in modesty is better, so if you feel like you may be addicted or find yourself constantly craving unhealthy foods, just try to take these steps to cut back; Get more sleep, or regulate your sleep schedule, make sure you're drinking plenty of water to stay hydrated, try lowering stress and anxiety levels, try chewing gum, or portion control your meals!
