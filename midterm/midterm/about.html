What You Should Know About Fitness!

Why is fitness so important? Why can't we just eat pizza and talk to our dogs all day? That's a good question! Physical Fitness has been around since the beginning of time- but back then it probably wasn't thought of as "fitness," but more so of just typical everyday life. This is because, before vehicles were invented humans had to walk everywhere and eventually invented the wagon and carriage. Fitness became more popular around the 70's with jogging and jazzercise, and since then has just gone up on the popular charts! A lot of people think of it as "a way of life," and not so much as a job that you have to work at.

Exercising is important for various reasons:

  1. 1. It improves your quality of sleep, which we all know is important.
  2. 2. It helps improve your memory and brain function, and improves overall heart health.
  3. 3. It increases your levels of serotonin(the happy chemical) in your brain, and reduces feelings of anxiety and depression.
  4. 4. Working out decreases your overall chance of health issues/doctor visits, and aids in weight management.
  5. 5. Overall, exercising increases your life span!

It's never too late to start your fitness journey!
